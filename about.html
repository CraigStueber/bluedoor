About Me

Hi, my name’s Megan, and I love to look for the wonder in the every day and think you are never too old to play make believe! My joy as an artist is to bring happiness to others, so I love to work with colors and create new worlds. I’ve always found nature to be a place of inspiration and peace.

One of my favorite activities when I’m not painting is to go on a hike with my family, and I’m always amazed at the wonders nature shows us every time. I’m also a huge fan of cooperative board games, the Walking Dead, and trying my hand at baking new recipes.

Born and raised in Richmond, my passion is to create a local online art collective, where artists can share and support each others talents. For more information on the Richmond Art Collective, or to share your art contact me.
